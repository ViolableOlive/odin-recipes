Gigi's Pasta Salad

Description

Wonderful summer salad to take along to picnics or potlucks. Also, a great side item for sandwiches or baked potato night. This recipe came from Gigi.

Ingredients

  • 3 c cooked pasta twists (one box)
  • 2 T salad seasoning
  • 1 c pepperoni
  • 1 c quartered cherry tomatoes
  • 1 c Swiss cheese (mozzarella)
  • 1/2 c chopped green pepper
  • 1/2 c chopped purple onion
  • 1 c sliced olives
  • 1/4 t sugar
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • Salt and pepper
  • 1 sm. bottle of Italian dressing

Salad Seasoning ingredients

  • 1.5 t sesame seeds, ground
  • 1 t paprika
  • 3/4 t salt
  • 1/2 t poppy seed
  • 1/2 t celery seed
  • 1/4 t garlic powder
  • 1/4 t black pepper
  • 1 dash cayenne pepper
  • 2 T Romano cheese, grated

Steps

  1. Combine all seasoning ingredients
  2. Combine all ingredients in a large bowl
  3. Pour dressing over mixture
  4. Toss to blend, cover and chill several hours
