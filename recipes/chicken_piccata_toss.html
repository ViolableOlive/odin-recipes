Chicken Piccata Pasta Toss

Description

This was a popular beach dish!

Ingredients

  • 1 1/3lbs chicken tenders
  • Salt and pepper
  • 2 T butter, divided
  • 2 T olive oil, divided
  • 1/2 C chopped onion
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 T flour
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • 1 1/2 C chicken broth
  • 1lb penne pasta

Steps

  1. Prepare pasta and set aside
  2. Cut chicken into 1 inch pieces
  3. Brown chicken in 1 T olive oil for 5-6 minutes
  4. Season with salt and pepper, remove from pan
  5. Add 1 T olive oil and 1 T butter, add garlic and onion
  6. Whisk in flour and cook for 2 minutes
  7. Whisk in broth and lemon juice, cook until sauce thickens
  8. Add chicken and heat through
  9. Toss pasta with chicken
